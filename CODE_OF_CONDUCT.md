# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in the Responsively App community a
welcoming, safe, and equitable experience for everyone.

Contributors, maintainers, and community members are expected to treat one
another with respect, professionalism, and empathy regardless of age, body
size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender
identity or expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status,
nationality, personal appearance, race, caste, color, religion, sexual
identity, sexual orientation, or other protected characteristics.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that help create a positive community include:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Giving and accepting constructive feedback gracefully
- Taking responsibility for mistakes and working to repair harm
- Prioritizing what is best for the project and its community
- Showing empathy toward other community members

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination in any form
- Trolling, insulting, or derogatory comments
- Personal, political, or bad-faith attacks
- Sexualized language, imagery, or unwelcome sexual attention
- Publishing someone else's private information without explicit permission
- Threatening or encouraging violence, self-harm, or other dangerous behavior
- Any other conduct that could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a
  professional or community setting

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in all project spaces, including issues, pull
requests, discussions, code review, chat spaces, and community events. It also
applies when an individual is representing the project in public spaces, such
as through official social channels, project events, or direct interactions on
behalf of Responsively App.

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this Code of
Conduct. Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code,
issues, and other contributions that are not aligned with this Code of Conduct,
and may take temporary or permanent moderation actions when appropriate.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it privately to the
project maintainers at `p.manoj.vivek@gmail.com`.

Please include relevant details such as links, screenshots, or context that may
help with review. Reports will be handled as promptly and confidentially as
possible. Public issue threads should not be used to report Code of Conduct
violations.

## Enforcement Guidelines

When reviewing reported incidents, maintainers will consider the impact of the
behavior, whether it is part of a pattern, and what action is needed to protect
the community. Responses may include:

- A private warning
- A request to stop the behavior
- Removal of content
- Temporary suspension from community spaces
- Permanent removal from the project community

## Attribution

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the Contributor Covenant and related
open-source community standards.
